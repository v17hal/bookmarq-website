How it works

From review to trusted rating

1

Reviewer writes

A reviewer assesses a book across all 10 criteria with written justifications. The app computes a weighted 0–10 score.

2

Moderator approves

A moderator reads the full review and publishes or rejects it with private feedback. Only approved reviews go public.

3

Readers discover

Readers browse a poster grid, search titles, open the consolidated score card, and like or share reviews.

4

Community grows

Points and tiers reward genuine participation; leaderboards celebrate the best books and contributors.
